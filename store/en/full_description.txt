Think you've mastered Tic Tac Toe? Think again.

Ghost Tac Toe reinvents the classic game with one simple rule change that transforms everything: each player can only have 3 marks on the board at once. Place your 4th mark, and your oldest one vanishes like a ghost. 👻

This one twist eliminates draws, creates dramatic reversals, and demands a completely new strategy. You can't just block — you have to think about which of your marks will disappear next, and plan around your opponent's vanishing marks too.

🎮 FEATURES

★ Ghost Mode — The core twist. Only 3 marks per player. Your oldest mark fades and vanishes when you place a 4th. Watch marks age from solid to ghostly so you always know what's about to disappear.

★ Chaos Mode — A random cell glows with lightning each turn. Win through that cell for bonus points. Adds an extra layer of risk and reward.

★ Smart AI with 3 Difficulty Levels
  • Easy — Perfect for learning the ghost mechanic
  • Medium — Blocks your wins and looks for its own
  • Hard — Full minimax AI that plans multiple moves ahead, accounting for ghost evictions

★ Stats Tracking — Track your wins, losses, draws, and win streaks across all difficulty levels

HOW TO PLAY

1. Players take turns placing X or O on a 3×3 grid
2. Get 3 in a row to win (horizontal, vertical, or diagonal)
3. In Ghost Mode: when you place your 4th mark, your oldest mark disappears
4. Marks visually fade as they age — full color → dim → ghostly
5. Plan ahead: your winning line might vanish before you complete it!
